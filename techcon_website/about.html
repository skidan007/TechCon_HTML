History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup for tech professionals in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it evolved into an international conference known for spotlighting innovation, networking, and future-forward thinking. Today, it attracts thousands of attendees worldwide.

Mission

Our mission is to connect global innovators, foster collaborative growth, and inspire the next generation of technology leaders. We strive to provide a platform for sharing bold ideas, solving real-world problems, and shaping the future with inclusive innovation.

Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Chen

An AI ethics pioneer, Dr. Chen delivered a powerful keynote in 2022 on responsible AI development. She is renowned for her work at OpenLogic Labs, helping define global ethical standards for artificial intelligence.

Dan Vick

Raj Patel, a renowned cybersecurity expert, presented in 2019 on data sovereignty and privacy. His work has shaped legislation in multiple countries regarding digital rights and secure computing.

Linda Gomez

Linda inspired the 2021 audience with her vision for sustainable tech. Her company EcoSys has developed energy-efficient computing solutions now adopted by major data centers around the world.
